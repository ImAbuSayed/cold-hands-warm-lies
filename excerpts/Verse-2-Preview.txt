Pakkiya main yaariyan nibhaunda with my solid blood
Oh tu khed gayi khed sadi in the rainy flood
Ajj sadka te tyre ghasaunda main ruling the dark
Par tere sohne mukhde de agge losing my spark
Pital di goli de wangu piercing my soul
Main sab kuch lutaya tera playing the fool
Mera ik hath steering te holding the pain
Main kalla kalla jalda in the pouring rain
